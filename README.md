## ğŸ› Common Pests in Kolkata

### 1. Cockroaches
- **German Cockroach**: Kitchen and bathroom infestations
- **American Cockroach**: Drainage and basement areas
- **Prevention**: Seal cracks, fix leaks, regular cleaning

### 2. Termites
- **Subterranean Termites**: Attack from soil
- **Drywood Termites**: Infest furniture directly
- **Signs**: Mud tubes, hollow wood, discarded wings

### 3. Rodents
- **House Rats**: Common in residential areas
- **Bandicoots**: Sewerage and garden areas
- **Prevention**: Block entry points, remove food sources
